## Design Decisions

- Cleanup deletes the migration record (not just marks completed) to keep the store clean
- ResumeMigration treats missing record as success — this handles the "already completed" case gracefully
- Rollback on switch failure attempts to restart source container; if that also fails, the error is returned
- Source container is stopped before updating instance_id, then destroyed after — ensures no split-brain
